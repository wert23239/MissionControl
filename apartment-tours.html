Search rules

  • Prioritize Williamsburg/Northside listings with strong resale, light/common-charge sanity, and tax-abatement clarity.
  • For 1BRs, compare against 34 N7 #5D: ~$1.07M / 690 ft² / ~$1,550 per ft².
  • For 2BRs, be picky: under ~$1.35M is interesting; above that needs real upside.
  • Before offer: confirm pre-approval, building finances, abatement cliff, assessments, litigation, and exact buyer-agent fee terms.

Offer checklist

  • Pre-approval letter matched to offer price / loan amount.
  • Attorney ready before signing anything binding.
  • Comp set: same building, same neighborhood, same tax-abatement status.
  • Building diligence: financials, board minutes, assessments, litigation, insurance, reserves.
  • Mortgage-interest deduction modeled correctly: deduction reduces taxable income, not taxes dollar-for-dollar.
